Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all tools built under Free IT Tools, including the Blog Post Generator, Text to Image Tool, and ROI Calculator. These apps are provided as free, personal-use tools. Your privacy is respected and protected.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, log, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your IP address or location data
  • Browser fingerprints, cookies, or tracking identifiers
  • Text prompts, search queries, or inputs you submit
  • Generated content, images, or results
  • Usage behavior, session recordings, or analytics

How Your Inputs Are Handled

The Blog Post Generator and Text to Image Tool route your inputs through a lightweight server solely to securely communicate with third-party AI APIs. This protects API credentials from being exposed in the browser. Your inputs are:

  • Transmitted in real time to the relevant API
  • Never written to disk or stored in any database
  • Never logged, monitored, or reviewed by us
  • Discarded immediately after the API response is returned to your browser

The ROI Calculator performs all calculations entirely within your browser. No data is transmitted anywhere.
